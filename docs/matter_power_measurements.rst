Matter current measurements
############################

.. contents::
   :local:
   :depth: 2

In the |REPO_NAME|, the Matter configuration is inherited from the |NCS|.
The application does not introduce Matter-specific power tuning beyond what the |NCS| provides.

Overview
********

For detailed information about the power consumption of a Matter over Thread device, including measurement methodology and battery life considerations, see the white paper `Matter over Thread: Power consumption and battery life`_.

You can also use the `Online Power Profiler for Matter over Thread`_ to estimate power consumption and battery life for Matter over Thread configurations.
